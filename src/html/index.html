The Chateau Mar Beach Resort introduces a refreshing blend of modern simplicity and understated elegance to "the world's most famous beach". The Chateau Mar Beach Resort provides the perfect mix of style and comfort, along with a personalized quality of service aimed at enhancing your travel experience.
